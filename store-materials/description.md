# Google Play Store Materials

## English

### App Title
Qaza Tracker: Namaz & Fasting

### Short Description
Easily track and complete your missed (Qaza) prayers and Ramadan fasting.

### Long Description
Qaza Tracker is a simple, beautiful, and completely offline tool designed to help you organize and track your missed (Qaza) prayers and fasting days.

In our busy lives, it's easy to lose track of what we need to make up. Qaza Tracker provides a clean interface to:
- Set your missed periods for Namaz and Fasting.
- Track progress for each of the five daily prayers (plus Witr).
- Use the Qasr (traveler) prayer mode when applicable.
- Track missed Ramadan months and individual fasting days.
- Export and Import your data to keep it safe across devices.
- Visual progress tracking with growing trees and flowers.

Completely Offline: Your data remains private and stored only on your device.

Download Qaza Tracker today and start your journey towards completing your debts with ease.

Qaza Tracker: Намаз и Пост

Легко отслеживайте и восполняйте свои пропущенные (Каза) намазы и посты Рамадана.
